1st Guru Nanak Sacred Forest

1st Guru Nanak Sacred Forest was created near Bathinda and announced at the EcoSikh press conference in Chandigarh on 14th March 2019. In 24 months of time, we created 303 micro forests of 550 plants and These forests record at least 95% of survival rate.
